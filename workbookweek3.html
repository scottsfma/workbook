Hunt n' Gather - examples of noteworthy WEB ARCHIVES or GALLERIES OF WORKS

Google Arts & Culture
The website allows users to explore artworks, artifacts, and cultural heritage sites virtually.


Project Gutenberg
Project Gutenberg offers over 60,000 free eBooks, including classic literature, historical texts, and reference works. Users can download these books in various formats for free


The Met Collection
The Metropolitan Museum of Art's website offers access to thousands of artworks from its collection, including paintings, sculptures, decorative arts, and more. Users can explore artworks, read descriptions, and view high-resolution images
